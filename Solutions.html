SOLUTIONS

DIGITAL WATERMARK

DataPatrol Digital Watermark Solution applies the watermark to all end users machines and servers with detailed
information about the logged in user, IP Address, time, and other metadata.
With DataPatrol Digital watermark you get to set up granular policy
control for different groups to apply the watermark with specific looks for different groups.

ANTICOPY FEATURE

The AntiCopy feature from
DataPatrol allows administrators to control (Enable & Disable) the Copy of texts feature on your windows based
machines. If the feature is enabled, users won’t be allowed to utilize the copy of any texts, and this can be
enabled only when specific process is running.
